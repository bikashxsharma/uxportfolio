How I define myself as a Designer?

I have adopted design thinking as a mantra of life. I connect every problem as a design problem. I believe in a philosophy of design by questioning. A good question can lead to the depth of a problem and later to better solution.

I follow the double diamond model which consists of divergent and convergent. At first, I go for quantity to create choices and later shift to better quality to make choices.

For me, UX design is the process of making a usable product that is made with a collective effort between designers, stakeholders, and users. So, I avoid falling in love with my solution. Rather, I fall in love with the process and users to find the best solutions together.

I am most interested in user research, understanding human psychology behind using the product, rapid prototyping, and social psychology. At the moment, I am learning about accessibility and how to make product usable for most of the users despite their different backgrounds.
